    John Wick 1

    John Wick is a 2014 American neo-noir action thriller film directed by Chad Stahelski and David Leitch, in their directorial debuts, and written by Derek Kolstad. It stars Keanu Reeves, Michael Nyqvist, Alfie Allen, Adrianne Palicki, Bridget Moynahan, Dean Winters, Ian McShane, John Leguizamo, Willem Dafoe, and Lance Reddick. The film follows a retired hitman who seeks vengeance after his dog is murdered.

    John Wick 2

    John Wick: Chapter 2 is a 2017 American neo-noir action thriller film directed by Chad Stahelski and written by Derek Kolstad. It is the second installment in the John Wick film series, following John Wick (2014). The film stars Keanu Reeves, Common, Laurence Fishburne, Riccardo Scamarcio, Ruby Rose, John Leguizamo, Ian McShane, and Lance Reddick. It follows the titular character as he seeks vengeance for the death of his dog.

    John Wick 3

    John Wick: Chapter 3 Parabellum is a 2019 American neo-noir action thriller film directed by Chad Stahelski and written by Derek Kolstad. It is the third installment in the John Wick film series, following John Wick (2014) and John Wick: Chapter 2 (2017). The film stars Keanu Reeves, Halle Berry, Ian McShane, Laurence Fishburne, Mark Dacascos, Asia Kate Dillon, Lance Reddick, Saïd Taghmaoui, Jerome Flynn, Jason Mantzoukas, Tobias Segal, Boban Marjanović, and Anjelica Huston. It follows the titular character as he seeks vengeance for the death of his dog.

    John Wick 4

    John Wick: Chapter 4 is an upcoming American neo-noir action thriller film directed by Chad Stahelski and written by Derek Kolstad. It is the fourth installment in the John Wick film series, following John Wick (2014), John Wick: Chapter 2 (2017), and John Wick: Chapter 3 – Parabellum (2019). The film stars Keanu Reeves, Halle Berry, Ian McShane, Laurence Fishburne, Mark Dacascos, Asia Kate Dillon, Lance Reddick, Saïd Taghmaoui, Jerome Flynn, Jason Mantzoukas, Tobias Segal, Boban Marjanović, and Anjelica Huston. It follows the titular character as he seeks vengeance for the death of his dog.
